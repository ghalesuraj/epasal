== Description ==
Springoo, an extraordinary WordPress theme designed exclusively for fashion stores. Get ready to engage your users and immerse them in an unforgettable online experience. it's a gateway to showcase your trendiest collections and captivate your audience. With its stunning and modern design, Springoo becomes the perfect companion to bring your fashion store to life and enthrall your users.

For more information about Springoo please go to https://springoo.com/elementor-themes/ayyash/

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance -> Themes and click the 'Add New' button.
2. Type Springoo in the search form and press the 'Enter' key in your keyboard.
3. Click on the 'Activate' button to use your new theme right away.
